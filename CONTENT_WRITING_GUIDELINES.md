## Brand Voice & Positioning

### Core Positioning

**We are:** A startup MVP development agency that builds revenue-ready products in 20 days

**We are NOT:** A generic web development agency or freelancer marketplace

### Target Audience

**Primary:** Indian startup founders with:
- Budget: ₹85K - ₹3L+
- Timeline: 20-60 days
- Stage: Pre-launch or early-stage
- Need: Revenue-ready MVP

**Secondary:**
- International founders targeting Indian market
- Corporate innovation teams
- Entrepreneurs with validated ideas

### Brand Voice Characteristics

✅ **DO:**
- Founder-focused language
- Revenue and business outcome emphasis
- Transparent pricing
- Specific timelines (20 days)
- Startup terminology
- Action-oriented CTAs

❌ **DON'T:**
- Generic "we build websites" messaging
- Vague timelines ("fast delivery")
- Hidden pricing
- Corporate jargon
- Passive language

### Tone Examples

**Good:**
> "Launch your startup MVP in 20 days. Revenue-ready products from ₹85K. Book your discovery call today."

**Bad:**
> "We provide quality web development services. Contact us for a quote."

**Good:**
> "Built for founders who need to validate fast and start generating revenue."

**Bad:**
> "We create beautiful websites for businesses of all sizes."

## Keyword Strategy

### Primary Keywords (High Priority)

**Transactional Intent:**
- MVP development agency India
- Startup MVP development
- Build MVP in 20 days
- MVP development cost India
- Best MVP agency India
- Hire MVP developers India

**Service-Specific:**
- SaaS development India
- AI automation services India
- Mobile app development Delhi
- Web app development India
- Startup consulting India

**Geographic:**
- MVP development Delhi
- Startup development India
- App development Delhi NCR
- SaaS development Bangalore
- AI automation Mumbai

### Secondary Keywords

**Informational (Blog Content):**
- How to build an MVP
- MVP development cost
- MVP vs full product
- Startup launch checklist
- 20-day MVP framework

**Long-Tail:**
- How much does it cost to build MVP in India
- Best MVP development agency for startups
- Fastest way to launch startup MVP
- Revenue-ready MVP development
- Validated idea to MVP in 20 days

### Keyword Placement

**Title Tag:**
- Primary keyword at the beginning
- Include location if GEO page
- Keep under 60 characters
- Example: "MVP Development Delhi | Launch in 20 Days - RAGSPRO"

**Meta Description:**
- Primary + secondary keywords
- Include pricing/timeline
- Clear CTA
- 150-160 characters
- Example: "Build your startup MVP in 20 days. Revenue-ready products from ₹85K. Book discovery call with Delhi's top MVP agency."

**H1 Heading:**
- Primary keyword
- Benefit-focused
- Example: "Launch Your Startup MVP in 20 Days"

**Content:**
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Secondary keywords naturally throughout
- LSI keywords for context
- Keyword density: 1-2%

## Content Types

### 1. GEO Landing Pages

**Purpose:** Rank for location-specific searches

**Structure:**
```
H1: [Service] in [Location] | Launch in 20 Days
H2: Why Choose RAGSPRO for [Service] in [Location]
H2: Our [Service] Process
H2: [Location]-Based Success Stories
H2: Transparent Pricing for [Location] Startups
H2: Frequently Asked Questions
```

**Content Requirements:**
- 700+ words minimum
- Location mentioned 5-7 times naturally
- Service mentioned 8-10 times
- Include local testimonials
- Embed Google Maps
- Add Service schema with geographic data
- Multiple CTAs (top, middle, bottom)

**Example Opening:**
> "Looking for MVP development in Delhi? RAGSPRO helps Delhi-based startups launch revenue-ready MVPs in just 20 days. With transparent pricing from ₹85K and a proven track record of successful launches, we're Delhi's go-to MVP development agency."

### 2. Service Landing Pages

**Purpose:** Convert high-intent service searches

**Structure:**
```
H1: [Service] - Launch in 20 Days
H2: What You Get
H2: Our Process
H2: Pricing & Packages
H2: Success Stories
H2: Why Choose Us
H2: Get Started Today
```

**Content Requirements:**
- 800+ words
- Clear pricing ranges
- Specific deliverables
- Timeline breakdown
- Case studies with metrics
- Qualification form
- Calendar booking CTA

**Pricing Display:**
Always show transparent pricing:
- "From ₹85K for basic MVP"
- "₹1L - ₹2L for SaaS platforms"
- "₹3L+ for complex AI solutions"

### 3. Blog Posts

**Purpose:** Attract and educate potential customers

**Types:**

**A. Buyer-Intent Posts (High Priority)**
- "Best [Service] Agency in India 2024"
- "How Much Does [Service] Cost in India?"
- "[Service] Case Study: [Result]"
- "Top 10 [Service] Companies in India"

**B. Educational Posts (Medium Priority)**
- "How to Build an MVP in 20 Days"
- "MVP Development Complete Guide"
- "Startup Launch Checklist"
- "Idea Validation Framework"

**C. Thought Leadership (Low Priority)**
- "Future of Startup Development"
- "AI in MVP Development"
- "Building in Public: Our Journey"

**Structure:**
```
H1: [Keyword-Rich Title]
Introduction (100-150 words)
[BlogCTA position="after-intro"]

H2: Main Point 1
Content...

H2: Main Point 2
Content...
[BlogCTA position="mid-content"]

H2: Main Point 3
Content...

H2: Conclusion
Summary + CTA
[BlogCTA position="end"]
```

**Content Requirements:**
- 1500+ words for buyer-intent posts
- 1000+ words for educational posts
- Include case studies/examples
- Add internal links (3-5 per post)
- Include images/screenshots
- Add FAQ schema
- Multiple CTAs (3-4 per post)

### 4. Homepage Copy

**Hero Section:**
```
H1: Launch Your Startup MVP in 20 Days
Subheading: Revenue-ready MVPs for Indian startups. Transparent pricing from ₹85K. Built by founders, for founders.
CTA: Get Your MVP Roadmap
```

**Services Section:**
```
H2: Startup-Focused Development Services
- MVP Development: "Validate your idea fast"
- SaaS Development: "Build scalable platforms"
- AI Automation: "Automate and scale"
- Mobile Apps: "Reach users everywhere"
```

**Pricing Section:**
```
H2: Transparent Pricing for Startups
Subheading: No hidden costs. No surprises. Just honest pricing.
Price Range: Starting from ₹85K
CTA: View Detailed Pricing
```

## SEO Best Practices

### On-Page SEO Checklist

**Every Page Must Have:**
- [ ] Unique title tag (50-60 chars)
- [ ] Unique meta description (150-160 chars)
- [ ] H1 tag with primary keyword
- [ ] H2-H6 hierarchy
- [ ] Alt text on all images
- [ ] Internal links (3-5 per page)
- [ ] External links to authority sites
- [ ] Schema markup (appropriate type)
- [ ] Mobile-optimized
- [ ] Fast loading (<3 seconds)

### Content Structure

**Heading Hierarchy:**
```
H1: Main topic (only one per page)
  H2: Major section
    H3: Subsection
      H4: Detail point
```

**Paragraph Length:**
- 2-3 sentences per paragraph
- Use bullet points for lists
- Add white space for readability
- Break up long content with images

**Internal Linking:**
- Link to related services
- Link to relevant blog posts
- Link to GEO pages from homepage
- Use descriptive anchor text
- 3-5 internal links per page

**Example:**
> "Our [MVP development services](/mvp-development-services) help startups in [Delhi](/mvp-development-delhi) and across India launch in 20 days."

## Conversion Optimization

### CTA Best Practices

**Primary CTAs (High Intent):**
- "Get Your MVP Roadmap"
- "Book Discovery Call"
- "Start Your MVP Today"
- "Schedule Free Consultation"

**Secondary CTAs (Medium Intent):**
- "View Pricing"
- "See Case Studies"
- "Download Guide"
- "Learn More"

**CTA Placement:**
- Hero section (above fold)
- After introduction (blog posts)
- Mid-content (long pages)
- End of page
- Sticky button (mobile)

### Social Proof Elements

**Always Include:**
- Client testimonials with photos
- Case study metrics (conversion rates, revenue)
- Founder credentials (GitHub, LinkedIn)
- Project portfolio
- Client logos (if available)

**Metrics to Highlight:**
- "4.2% conversion rate"
- "₹50K MRR in 3 months"
- "10 hours/week saved"
- "50+ successful launches"

### Trust Signals

**Add Throughout Content:**
- Transparent pricing
- Specific timelines
- Real case studies
- Founder story
- Building in public
- GitHub projects
- YouTube content
- LinkedIn presence

## Examples

### Good vs Bad Headlines

**Good:**
- "Build Your SaaS MVP in 20 Days - From ₹1L"
- "Delhi's Top MVP Development Agency for Startups"
- "How We Built a ₹50K MRR MVP in 20 Days"

**Bad:**
- "Web Development Services"
- "Quality Software Solutions"
- "Contact Us for More Information"

### Good vs Bad CTAs

**Good:**
- "Get Your Free MVP Roadmap"
- "Book 30-Min Discovery Call"
- "See Pricing & Packages"

**Bad:**
- "Click Here"
- "Learn More"
- "Submit"

### Good vs Bad Descriptions

**Good:**
> "RAGSPRO builds revenue-ready MVPs for Indian startups in 20 days. Transparent pricing from ₹85K. 50+ successful launches. Book your discovery call today."

**Bad:**
> "We are a web development company offering various services. Contact us for more information about our solutions."
